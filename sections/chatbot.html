Skill Gap Chatbot Handbook

  • What to enter: List your current skills (e.g. Python, React, ML, Cloud, Mobile, Beginner, etc.), describe your experience, or ask for advice on healthcare SDE learning paths.
  • How to describe: Include tools, languages, frameworks, or experience level ("I am a beginner with Python and want to work in healthcare software").
  • What the chatbot does: It analyzes your input and suggests learning paths, skills to add, and next steps relevant to the medical sector.
Examples you can enter:
  • "I know Python and AWS, but want to work in hospital software."
  • "I am a beginner with React and want to specialize in medical web apps."
  • "I have experience in machine learning but no knowledge of healthcare standards."
  • "What should I learn next for a career in mobile health app development?"
Typical Output:
  • Recommended skills and frameworks to add (e.g., FHIR, HL7, HIPAA, TensorFlow).
  • Suggested next learning steps or courses.
  • Advice on market demand and employability for healthcare SDEs.
  • Tips for building a portfolio tailored for medical software roles.
